# Retail Customer Intelligence Analytics

An end-to-end data analytics project focused on analyzing customer shopping behavior, purchase trends, loyalty patterns, and revenue-driving factors using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

The project demonstrates how businesses can use consumer data to improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and increase long-term customer retention.

---

# Project Overview

This project analyzes retail customer shopping data to uncover:
- purchasing trends
- customer segmentation
- loyalty behavior
- discount impact
- subscription performance
- shipping preferences
- revenue-driving factors

The workflow simulates a real-world business analytics process involving:
- data preparation
- SQL-based analysis
- dashboard visualization
- business reporting
- strategic recommendations

---

# Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve:
- customer engagement
- sales performance
- retention
- marketing effectiveness
- product positioning

The objective is to identify patterns in customer demographics, purchasing habits, and transaction behavior to support data-driven business decisions.

---

# Key Insights

- Subscription users contributed significantly higher revenue
- Express shipping customers had higher average purchase value
- Female customers generated slightly higher total revenue
- Discount users still showed strong spending behavior
- Loyal customers represented the highest-value segment
